About Me

I have 15+ years of experience at the intersection of computational biology, genomics, and cloud infrastructure. I help biotech companies turn complex scientific problems into technical solutions, creating scalable bioinformatics pipelines, AI workflows, and cloud-native systems across AWS and high-performance computing environments. I'm passionate about building robust, automated platforms that accelerate discovery and improve patient outcomes.

My work has supported clinical screening of thousands of breast and ovarian cancer patients and empowered over 20 biotech and research clients with reliable tools and pipelines to address real-world scientific challenges.

I hold a PhD from the University of Oxford. After a postdoctoral role at the Wellcome Centre for Human Genetics, Oxford, and consulting for the Institute of Cancer Research, London, I served as IT Director and Senior Scientist at The Bioinformatics CRO for 7 years.
